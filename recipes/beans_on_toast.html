Beans on Toast

Enjoy this classic comfort food at any time of the day - breakfast, lunch, dinner, or even as a midnight snack! Grated cheese melts enticingly over conforting baked beans poured on hearty buttered white bread.

Ingredients:

  • 1 can of baked beans in tomato sauce
  • 2 slices of white farmhouse-style bread
  • Pat of butter (melted)
  • 60g of mature cheddar cheese (grated)
  • Salt/pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Heat the beans in a pan on the stove, stirring regularly until they are nearly boiling.
  2. Toast the bread in a toaster or grill until golden brown.
  3. Butter the toast evenly using the melted butter and place the slices on a serving plate.
  4. Pour the heated beans over the buttered toast.
  5. Sprinkle the grated cheddar cheese over the beans.
  6. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve immediately.
